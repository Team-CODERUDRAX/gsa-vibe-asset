Who We Are

Not a Club.
A Movement.

The Gemini Builders Community is a student-led initiative at United Institute of Technology, Prayagraj — built under the Google Student Ambassador program. We exist for one reason: to turn curious students into confident AI builders.

No gatekeeping. No prior experience required. Just bring your curiosity and your willingness to break things and learn from it.

We run hands-on sessions, ship real projects, and grow together — one build at a time.
